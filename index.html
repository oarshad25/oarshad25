Welcome! I am a computational biologist applying statistical and bioinformatic approaches to analyse, model and interpret high-throughput biological data. Currently, I am a postdoctoral fellow in Computational Genomics in the Hurles group at the Wellcome Sanger Institute in Cambridge, England.

I completed my PhD in genomic signal processing at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Texas A&M University in 2017. Prior to joining Sanger, I was a postdoctoral fellow in computational cancer biology at The U.S. Department of Energy's Pacific Northwest National Laboratory.
